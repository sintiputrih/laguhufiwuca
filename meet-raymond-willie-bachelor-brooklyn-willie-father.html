Raymond Willie Job

Per the LinkedIn profile, Raymond is a director of sales operations at Velcro Companies in Manchester, New Hampshire. He started the job there in December 2021. Before that, from 2019 to 2021, Raymond was employed at Targus as director of Global Sales Operations.

Furthermore, from November 2018 to December 2019, Raymond Willie was working for GENLED Brands as Global Head of Sales Operations. In 2018 from April to October, Ultraviolet International hired him as Vice President of Sales in Dallas, Texas.

Raymond started his career in 1980 at Willow Distributors, Inc. He worked in the company for 17 years till 1997. Following ten years after that, he worked at DataTegrity, Inc. in the position of Vice President of Sales Operations.

From 2016 to 2017, he worked at Opulent Americas as Vice President of Channel Sales.
