Lil Uzi Vert Net Worth: Lifestyle &amp; Watches

Publish date: 2024-09-16

Lil Uzi Vert’s net worth is about $25 million as an American rapper, singer, and songwriter.

Uzi is famous for his asexual fashion sense, facial tattoos, piercings, peculiar hairstyles, and also a melodic emo approach to trap.

Lil Uzi gained recognition following the release of his mixtape Luv Is Rage in 2015, which led to a recording deal with Atlantic Records.

Likewise, he gained mainstream attention with the release of his debut single in 2016 titled “Money Longer.”

Similarly, after releasing several mixtapes, including the one with fellow rapper Gucci Mane, Vert made it to the Billboard Hot 100 on the hit single “Bad and Boujee” featuring Migos.

Furthermore, he won the MTV Video Music Award for Song of Summer for his first top ten single, “XO Tour Llif3”.

The single “XXO Tour Llif3” from debut studio album Luv Is Rage 2, released in 2017, which ranked at the number 1 on the Billboard 200, was certified double platinum by the RIAA.

Lil Uzi was nominated for Best New Artist at the 2018 Grammy Awards.

2020 Eternal Atake, his second studio album, was among the most anticipated albums in trap rap.

Similarly, his album Eternal Atake also debuted at number one on the Billboard 200.

Early Life

Lil Uzi Vert, originally Symere Bysil Woods, was born on July 31st, 1995, in North Philadelphia’s Francisville neighborhood.

Lil grew up listening to Ying Yang Twins and Mike Jones and later started listening to Wiz Khalifa and Meek Mil, whose influence can be seen in his style.

He is not just into Hip Hop. Reportedly, Lil is a huge fan of Marilyn Manson and has even called Mechanical Animals his favorite album.

Similarly, he listened to Paramore, Smash Mouth, My Chemical Romance, The Rocket Summer, Simian, and The All-American Rejects when he was just 13 years of age.

He began rapping at 10th grade.

Besides, that Uzi, along with Aston and other friends, created a rap group at Steaktown. The group broke up when Lil was 17 years of age.

Lil Uzi was kicked out of his house by his mother after he dropped out of school and quit his job at the Bottom Dollar store after four days of working.

The overall situation got out of hand, and Uzi got his face tattoo, the word “Faith” under his hairline, which forced him to take rap career ore seriously.

Watches

Lil Uzi Vert has a massive watch collection. Let’s face it, everyone likes to have finer things in life, and Lil Uzi is no exception.

His collection includes timepieces from world-renowned brands, mostly from Swiss makers.

First of all, he has a Rolex Sky-Dweller in Stainless steel with pave invisible honeycomb setting and a blue dial. The watch reference number 326934 costs about $55,000.

Likewise, another of his impressive and prized timepieces is his custom 41mm diamond-encrusted Audemars Piguet Royal Oak Skeleton. The watch is wrapped in 18k rose gold with an untouched dial showcasing its skeleton movement.

The value of the watch is an eye-watering $300,000.
