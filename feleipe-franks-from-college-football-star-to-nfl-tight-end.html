Feleipe Franks: From College Football Star to NFL Tight End

Publish date: 2024-07-19

Feleipe Franks, born on December 22, 1997, is an American football player who has made a remarkable journey from being a college football star to becoming a tight end for the Atlanta Falcons in the National Football League (NFL). This injury not only ended his season but also led to his transfer to the University of Arkansas.

Franks faced a significant setback after his injury, but he refused to let it define his career. Determined to make a comeback, he worked tirelessly to regain his strength and improve his skills.
