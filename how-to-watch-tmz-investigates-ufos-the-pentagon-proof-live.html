How To Watch TMZ Investigates: UFOs: The Pentagon Proof Live

Publish date: 2024-07-06

The truth is out there… and apparently it’s airing tonight on Fox!

Executive produced by Harvey Levin, TMZ Investigates: UFOs: The Pentagon Proof is a one-hour special analyzing the U.S. government’s official response to conversations about the existence of UFOs. Featuring never-before-seen footage of unidentified flying objects and, as Sean L. McCarthy reports, “interviews with U.S. Senators, military pilots, former Pentagon officials, a Harvard scientist, as well as the two people who pushed the federal government to finally acknowledge the U.S. has been studying the UFO phenomena for decades,” the special will be broadcast on Fox and available for next-day streaming on Hulu.

Here’s how to watch TMZ Investigates: UFOs: The Pentagon Proof live online.

WHAT TIME IS TMZ INVESTIGATES: UFOS: THE PENTAGON PROOF ON TONIGHT?

TMZ’s special airs Tuesday, June 29 from 8:00-9:00 p.m. ET on Fox.

HOW TO WATCH TMZ INVESTIGATES: UFOS: THE PENTAGON PROOF LIVE ONLINE:

If you have a valid cable login, you can watch TMZ Investigates: UFOS: The Pentagon Proof live on Fox.com and the FOX NOW app (available on iTunes, Amazon, and Google Play). Depending on your location, you can also access a Fox live stream with an active subscription to fuboTV, Sling TV, Hulu + Live TV, YouTube TV, or AT&T TV NOW.

The special will also be available for next-day streaming on Hulu, the Fox website and Fox app.

CAN I WATCH TMZ INVESTIGATES: UFOS: THE PENTAGON PROOF LIVE ON HULU?

Yes! If you have an active subscription to Hulu + Live TV, you can watch tonight’s special live or on demand via the service’s Fox live stream. Hulu offers a seven-day free trial for eligible subscribers.

WILL TMZ INVESTIGATES: UFOS: THE PENTAGON PROOF BE ON HULU?

Yes! TMZ Investigates: UFOs: The Pentagon Proof will be available for next-day streaming on Hulu beginning Wednesday, June 30.
