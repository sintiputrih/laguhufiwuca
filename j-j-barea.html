Who is J? J. Barea?

Jose Juan Barea Mora, also known as J. J. Barea, is a professional basketball player from Puerto Rico who most recently played for the NBA’s Dallas Mavericks (NBA). The Dallas Mavericks waived him on December 10, 2020. He started his basketball career at Northeastern University before joining the Mavericks in 2006, making him the eighth Puerto Rican player in the NBA. Among other leagues, he has played in the NBA Development League and the Baloncesto Superior Nacional. In 2006 and 2010, he was a member of the Puerto Rican national team that won gold in the Central American and Caribbean Games. As a point guard for Puerto Rico, they earned gold in the 2011 Pan American Games and silver in the 2007 Pan American Games. He was hired head coach of the BSN’s Indios de Mayaguez on May 30, 2017.
