Meet Girl Named Tom Members from ‘The Voice’

Girl Named Tom includes three siblings; Bekah, Joshua, and Caleb Liechty.

The trio hails from Ohio and was born to their mother, Holly in Pettisville.

They have yet to introduce their father as of now.

Out of the three, Caleb is the eldest as he is currently 26 years old followed by Joshua and Bekah who are 24 and 21 years old respectively.

The Liechty family resides in South Bend, Ohio, and are very supportive of each other.

All of the Liechty siblings wanted to become doctors however, all three dropped their dreams when their father was diagnosed with a rare type of cancer.

Thus, everyone settled at home to spend some more quality family time.

Hence, they formed Girl Named Tom in 2019 and traveled around the United States.

According to Hollywood Life, the band has performed in 67 shows in 27 different cities.

As they have finally reached the finale of “The Voice” season 21, their dreams of a mainstream career are finally on view.

Moreover, with time, the band has collected a significant amount of fans.
