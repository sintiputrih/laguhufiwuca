Who is Kanye West rumored girlfriend Juliana Nalu?

Publish date: 2024-03-14

Source: IG

  • Juliana Nalu is currently rumored to be dating the rapper Kanye West
  • Nalu is a model from Complexo de Chapadao, which is north of Rio De Janeiro
  • Nalu has known that she wants to be a model since young

The 45-years old Kanye West was recently spotted with a mystery woman on a movie date. Well, the mystery woman has now been revealed as Brazilian model Juliana Nalu.

Ye and Juliana hit up a movie theater in early October in Hollywood as the two went to see a movie, “Triangle of Sadness“. This is not the first time the two have been spotted together as they checked into a hotel together on Thursday in Beverly Hills.

Similarly, Ye and Juliana were seen together on Saturday dinner night at Giorgio Baldi in Santa Monica. However, the lovely pair hasn’t yet confirmed their relationship status. Also, it is unclear how they truly are to one another.

A Native of Rio de Janeiro

Juliana Nalu was born on October 17, 1998, and grew up in Complexo de Chapadao, which is north of Rio De Janeiro.

She was raised by a single mother after her parents divorced. She has also a brother and other three half-siblings, from her father’s side. Nalu, who has now gained international recognition, has always wanted to do major things outside of Brazil.

She claimed that even though she had no idea how to achieve her dream, she “knew that she could try to reach [it]”.

Juliana Nalu has always wanted to be a model since she was a child

Juliana Nalu knew that she want to be a model since she was six. In an interview with Latina magazine, she said that by the age of 19, she had relocated to Los Angeles with her mother for beauty and modeling jobs.

She stated,

“My mom was helping me follow my dreams, traveling to agencies with me, and making brand connections. I lived in the favelas with my family until I was 18,”

“It was hard to deal with the violence, and I didn’t have access to many privileges in Brazil, compared to others. However, my mom had so much love for [and my siblings] and gave us everything we needed to be a good person.”

She joined ELITE Models management as soon as she arrived in Los Angeles. At the age of 18, she was also chosen to take part in the L’Oreal Paris Brazil advertisement. Additionally, Juliana has modeled for Yeezy, which is most likely how she first met rapper Kanye West.

She is presently signed to ELITE Models LA, ELITE Models NYS, and Mix Models Agency, according to her IG bio.

Similarly, she recently modeled for the clothing brand 6 PM at Milan Fashion Week. She is also the face of the summer 2023 Australian swimwear label FAE’s campaign.

As a model, Juliana stands at the height of 5 feet 9 inches and has a brown complexion. Her hair is brown, and her eye color is also brown.

Nalu is ruling social media after being spotted with Kanye West

The Brazilian model has amassed a huge fan base on her respective social media handles. While scrolling through her Instagram feed, the model can be seen posing in a variety of eye-catching outfits and showcasing her style in each photo on her Instagram posts.

Juliana also flaunted a pair of Yeezy sunglasses in one of her most recent Instagram posts. As if now she has more than 668k followers on her Instagram.

She is also active on Tiktok under the username @nalujuliana where she has over 449.1k followers. Similar to this, she has almost 2.2k followers on her Facebook page.
