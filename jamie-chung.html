Early Life and Education

Jamie Chung, born on April 10, 1983, in San Francisco, California, had a diverse upbringing that influenced her journey to success. Upon graduating from high school in 2001, Chung’s thirst for knowledge led her to pursue higher education. She enrolled at the University of California, Riverside, to study economics. During her time at university, she embraced the college experience by becoming a member of the Kappa Kappa Gamma sorority. This involvement further molded her social and leadership skills.

With unwavering dedication, Jamie Chung graduated in 2005 with a Bachelor of Arts degree in economics. Her academic achievements exemplify her strong work ethic and commitment to personal growth.

Chung’s early life experiences and education provided a strong foundation that allowed her to excel in her future endeavors.
