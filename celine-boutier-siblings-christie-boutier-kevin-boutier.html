Celine Boutier siblings: Christie Boutier, Kevin Boutier

Publish date: 2024-07-18

Celine Boutier (born 10 November 1993) is a French professional golfer. She competes on the United States-based LPGA Tour and is also a member of the Ladies European Tour (LET).

Boutier turned professional in 2016. In 2017, Boutier played on Symetra Tour where she won twice, winning the Self Regional Healthcare Foundation Classic and Sioux Falls GreatLIFE Challenge.
