How Sarah Colonna Made Her Fortune

Sarah Colonna was born in Wiesbaden, Germany, in December 1974 and grew up in Farmington, Arkansas. She studied at the University of Arkansas before relocating to Los Angeles, California, to pursue a comedy career.

She began performing stand-up comedy in bars and festivals before making it to the semifinals of NBC’s Last Comic Standing. On Chelsea Lately, a well-known talk show hosted by Chelsea Handler, she received her big break as a frequent guest and writer.
