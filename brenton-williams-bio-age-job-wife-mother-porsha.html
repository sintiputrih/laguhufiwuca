Who Is Brenton Williams? His Bio, Age, Job, Wife, Mother

Publish date: 2024-01-16

Brenton Williams is among the most low-key sibling of RHOA star Porsha Williams. Porsha and her sister Lauren along with their mother Diane T Williams earned fame as a social media star, but, Brenton appeared in the public eye only a few times. However, we’ve put together this Brenton Williams Bio to help you introduce Porsha’s younger brother to you.

This article covers information related to his age, his job, his wife, his mother, and his kids. So, scroll down this article to learn more about Brenton.

Meet Brenton Williams, Porsha Brother

‘Real Housewives Of Atlanta’ star Porsha Williams was one of the five children born between Hosea Williams II, Diane T Williams, and Andria ‘Lisa’ Williams. She and her brother Hosea Williams III was born to their mother Diane T Williams. But, her half-siblings Lauren Williams and Brenton were born to their mother, Andria ‘Lisa’ Williams.

Porsha witnessed the divorce of her parents at the age of 3. Porsha addressed the impact of the parents’ divorce in one of the episodes of the show. “It was just kind of confusing at first because I had my home with Mommy that felt complete, solid.” She explained. “And then over here with Dad was another home that could be complete and solid, and why am I going in between?”

Porsha spent most of her life with her mother while her half-siblings Lauren and Brenton got to the privilege of having both parents around. Lauren had explained: “We share the same dad. She’s lived with Daddy, and I more so came over on the weekends, but saying “half anything” would get you a spanking in the house.”

Brenton Williams Mother

Brenton Lorenzo Williams was born to his mother Andria ‘Lisa’ Williams. He is the younger brother of the RHOA star. According to her LinkedIn profile, Lisa worked for DeKalb County Government as an accounting manager since November 2009. Starting from March 2019, she has taken the role of a Controller.

Lisa holds an MBA degree from Brenau University in Business Administration and Management. After his father’s death, his mother Lisa and his step-mother Diane started bonding with each other.

Talking about her age, Andria Lisa  Williams is reportedly 58 years old and celebrates her birthday on 29 January. She currently resides in Stone Mountain, Georgia.

Does Brenton Williams Appear On Family Matters?

No, Brenton hasn’t appeared on Family Matters yet.

The casts who appeared on the shows are his two sisters Porsha and Lauren, his step-mother Diane, Porsha’s husband Simon, Dennis McKinley, Gina Clayton, Drew Sidora, Shamea Morton, Yandy Smith-Harris, and Jennifer Williams.

Brenton Williams Age

Several online sources revealed that Brenton Williams was 38 years old as of 2022.

Does Brenton Williams Have A Wife?

Brenton Williams is married to his wife Amanda Williams. He and his wife tied the knots on 18 September 2014. The couple celebrated their 7th wedding anniversary in September 2021. They are parents to four kids; three daughters (Acacia. Amaya, and Lily) and one son named Kai, all below the age of 7.

Their youngest daughter Amaya Ann Williams was born in May 2020. Lily turned 3 years old in early May 2021.

As for his wife, Amanda Williams is the owner/operator of Run About Clothing. Her business makes Handmade Children’s Clothing. She is on Facebook (@avpianomusic).

Brenton Williams Job

Porsha’s brother Brenton is currently the owner of Gaijin Racing which he founded in December 2019. He also owned SLV Auto Salvage in Del Norte, Colorado. His business has a YouTube channel as well as a Gaijin Racing website.

Brenton had started his career as Resource Technician at HP Computers where he worked from 2004 to 2005 in Houston Texas. Then, he also worked at Walmart Tire Lube Express as Automotive Technician from 2004-2005.

Starting from October 2005 to January 2010, Brenton worked for Hosea Williams Bonding Company as Bail Bonding Agent/Bail Recovery Agent/Collections Director in Decatur, Georgia. From 2012 to 2014, he worked for Lithium517 as an assistant store manager/store manager in Estes Park, Colorado.

Whereas, from 2014-2015, he worked for Estes Valley Parks and Recreation District as an assistant mechanic.

Talking about his education, Brenton enrolled and graduated from Langham Creek High School in 2002. Moreover, he took Motorcycle Technology Course at Dekalb Technical College.
