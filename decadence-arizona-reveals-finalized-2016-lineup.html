Decadence Arizona Reveals Finalized 2016 Lineup

Publish date: 2024-07-25

Recommended Videos

Decadence Arizona has released the finalized lineup for its upcoming end of the year festival, revealing a stacked roster filled to the brim with talent. Serving as one of the premiere New Year’s Eve festival brands, Decadence routinely impresses with their lineup reveals and the roster for Arizona’s 2016 event is no exception.

Decadence has secured some exciting headliners for the upcoming festival, including The Chainsmokers, deadmau5, DJ Snake, and Zedd, with DJ sets from Porter Robinson and Disclosure as well. Additionally, Jauz and Marshmello are listed as special guests.

The rest of the program is comprised of equally enticing talent, with names like Malaa, Slushii, Jackal, Kill Paris, Ookay, Tritonal, Vanic, Zomboy and more taking up prime real estate on the lineup.

With an impressive array of performers and a number of different styles represented across the roster, Decadence Arizona is shaping up to be one of the biggest New Year’s Eve events around. The festival will return to the Rawhide Town & Event Center on December 30 and 31, and you can head here to purchase tickets.
