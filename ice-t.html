Career and Professional Life

Profession-wise Ice-T is a very famous rapper today. He has worked on different projects and made his name one of the best in history. Initially, he joined the US Army in 1979 and worked for four years.

Talking about his works; he has around 8 studio albums, 3 live albums, 7 compilation albums, 5 video albums, 50 music videos, 1 extended play, 51 singles, and 1 soundtrack album.
