What Are The Allegations Against Ree Drummond? How Much Weight Did The American Blogger Lose?

Publish date: 2024-01-24

Ree Drummond is a 53 years old American blogger, author, food writer, photographer, and television personality, who made it to the No. 22 on Forbes’ Top 25 Web Celebrities in 2010.

Drummond was born in Bartlesville, Oklahoma, the United States, on the 6th of 1969, to her father, William Dale Smith, and her mother, Gerre Schwert.

Furthermore, the blogger is married to Ladd Drummond and has five children: Alex, Bryce, Paige, Jamar, and Todd Drummond; the couple resides on a working ranch outside Pawhuska, Oklahoma.

What Are The Allegations Against Ree Drummond?

Drummond got accused of making a racist joke on her Food Network cooking show in 2017, which sounded very offensive to Asians, while her husband loved classic Buffalo wings, and she pranked him by serving an Asian iteration.

The American blogger served a tray of delicious-looking Asian hot wings out of the oven, but her family asked for the actual chicken wings as they didn’t appear appetizing. Ree then retrieved a tray of American-style wings while joking she wouldn’t be that mean to them. It created hate for The Pioneer Woman presenter all over the internet, but other than that, she hasn’t gotten involved in any recent allegations.

Ree Drummond Weight Loss: Diet & Workout

Ree has lost a significant amount of weight in a year; as per prevention, the blogger lost 55 pounds in 2021, which is almost 25 kgs, and it wouldn’t have been easy for the 53-year-old.

As an owner of a restaurant/bakery, hosting a cooking show and having a cooking website, she’d make excuses for being too busy to exercise and eat healthily, putting on weight throughout the years.

However, Drummond hit rock bottom while one of her daughter’s weddings was approaching, and she decided to lose those weights. So she started exercising up to six days a week, and when she slowed down the pace, she still was doing the rowing machine, lifted weights, and Pilates to keep her body moving. Furthermore, she weighed her food, counted her calorie intake, and cut out added sugar and alcohol from her diet.
