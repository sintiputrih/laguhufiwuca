Career

Jagger made her first appearance on the fashion runway in 1998 when she walked alongside her mother for Thierry Mugler. In September 2002, Jagger has named the face of Lancôme’s LCM cosmetics line. She was chosen to represent Mango, a Spanish fashion chain, in 2005. She was named the face of the New Skinny Lingerie Campaign in January 2013. In 2013, Jagger appeared in a Wrangler Jeans campaign. Jagger posed for Redken in 2016.
