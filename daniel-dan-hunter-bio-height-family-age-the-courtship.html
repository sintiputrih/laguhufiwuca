Daniel “Dan” Hunter joined NBC’s dating show The Courtship to experience what it was like dating in (1811 to 1820). He moved to England to film the show and has successfully made it to the next episode after completing several challenges. But who is he outside of the show?

We bring you information on his family, height, age, and job. Keep reading to learn more.

Daniel “Dan” Hunter On The Courtship

Daniel “Dan” Hunter made his TV show debut starring in NBC’s dating series The Courtship as one of the suitors. He is competing to win the heart of “the princess” Nicole Remy hence he competes with other good men vying with the same purpose. All of this happens in Regency-era England setting.

The first episode that premiered has already premiered that ended with the elimination of the three suitors Dr. Jarrett Schanzer, Lewis Echavarria, and Caleb Ward.

On 15 February 2022 before the premiere of the show, Daniel announced on his Instagram, “Who says fairytales don’t exist? I went to a castle in England in search of love, romance, and adventure. I planned to sweep a breathtaking princess off her feet and ride into the sunset. But so did the 15 other guys. Tune in to @nbc to see how it all went down! #thecourtship premiers on March 6th at 8/7c”.

During the time of the filming, Daniel also forged friendships with his fellow suitors. He posted a photo of Christian Lee Cones and Derek Jacob Kesseler while boasting about his friendship.

Daniel “Dan” Hunter Relationship Status

Daniel “Dan” Hunter Family

Daniel Hunter was born to his parents Matthew (IG: @jamesmatthewhunter & FB: @matt.hunter.31521) and Renee Daniels-Hunter (IG: @reneedanielshunter & FB: @renee.danielshunter). His father is a graduate of Pasadena High School and later attended Optometry at the University of Houston.

Matt currently works at Perich Eye Center. His mother is Marathon, Florida native who turned 71-year-old in June 2021. Daniel’s parents have been married for over 37 years and celebrated their anniversary in March 2022.

Daniel grew up on a boat from the age of 9 until he was 17. Sailing around the world with his parents and his sister, he lived a remarkable childhood.

On Father’s Day 2020, Daniel posted on his Instagram, “Happy Father’s Day to the legend who taught me everything I know. Love you pops. Here’s to still kicking ass in beach volleyball doubles”.

In his family, Daniel also has a sister named Nicole. She celebrates her birthday on 1 March every year. Nicole and Daniel lost the 94-year-old grandmother in March 2021.

Daniel “Dan” Hunter Job

Daniel “Dan” Hunter is the owner of a travel company named Now Or Never Trips (@nowornevertrips). The company takes pride in a passion for travel into a lifestyle. His company was established to provide an unforgettable, bucket-list experience to meet new people, see exotic destinations, and live life to the absolute fullest.

The company works on a mission to make the vacation as simple & seamless as possible. Each itinerary allowed people to meet from all over the world and leave with lifelong friendships. The company promises a surreal setting with adventurous excursions, top-notch accommodations, and crazy parties.

His company supported his journey to England and starred in NBC’s dating show The Courtship.

However, Daniel started his career as an account manager at Gartner in 2012. After working in that role from 2015 to 2016, he became account executive – Major Accounts. From 2016 to 2017, he worked for Salesforce as Senior Account Executive after leaving Gartner.

After founding his company, Daniel again joined Gartner in 2019 as Business Development Director and has been working there ever since. Daniel holds a bachelor’s degree from the University of Florida – Warrington College of Business and a master’s degree in International Business from the same. He also attended and studied as an exchange student at Esade, one of Europe’s top business schools.

Fun Fact: Daniel was homeschooled until he got enrolled in a Spanish boarding school.
