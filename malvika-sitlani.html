Wiki/Biography

Malvika Sitlani Aryan [1] was born on Saturday, 1 August 1992 (age 28 years; as of 2020) in Dubai. Her zodiac sign is Leo. She lived in Dubai upto class 2, and later, she moved to Mumbai. She did her schooling at Bai Avabai Framji Petit Girls’ High School in Mumbai. She pursued Bachelor of Mass Media at R. D. National & W. A. Science College in Mumbai. [2]

Parents & Siblings

Her father’s name is Deepak Sitlani. Her mother, Jenifer Caroline, is a former model and food blogger. Malvika has two brothers, Jay and Jake. Her younger brother, Jake, is the founder of a creative agency ‘Crash Boom Bang,’ and he is also a YouTuber.

Relationships, Husband & Children

After dating her boyfriend Akhil Aryan for more than ten years, the couple got engaged in November 2020 at Escobar, Bandra. Later, the couple did a court marriage in the presence of their family members only. [4]

Career

In 2011, she started her career as a model. She participated in the beauty pageant ‘Femina Miss India 2012,’ and she has won the titles PCJ Femina Miss Timeless Beauty and Pune Marriott Femina Miss Congeniality Model.

Malvika Sitlani in Pond’s Femina Miss India

In 2015, she participated in the supermodel TV hunt ‘India’s Next Top Model,’ and she was eliminated in the 7th episode.

Malvika Sitlani in India’s Next Top Model

In 2015, she started her YouTube channel and uploaded various makeup videos on her channel.

Malvika Sitlani’s different makeup looks

She gained immense popularity with her makeup videos and has over 669K subscribers on YouTube (as of January 2021). She played the role of Ayushmann Khurrana‘s girlfriend in the Hindi film ‘Meri Pyaari Bindu’ (2017).
