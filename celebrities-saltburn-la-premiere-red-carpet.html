Celebrities at the Saltburn LA Premiere: Margot Robbie &amp; More

Publish date: 2024-05-15

Red carpet premieres are finally back (and better than ever)!

Back on Nov 14, 2023, so many stars arrived in style for the Los Angeles, California premiere of Saltburn, specifically arriving at The Theatre at Ace Hotel.

Related story Keira Knightley’s Super-Rare Public Appearance Shows She’s Forever a Chanel It-Girl

For those who won’t know, Saltburn is a psychological thriller comedy that follows a group of family members at a sprawling estate, who end up being a rather peculiar set of people that make a wicked summer.

Starring Barry Keoghan, Jacob Elordi, Rosamund Pike, and Carey Mulligan, this star-studded cast will take part in the highly-anticipated film that’ll come out on Nov 22, 2023. Plus, it’s directed and written by Emerald Fennell (and produced by Tom Ackerley and Margot Robbie)!

But back to the fashion: from Robbie’s ultra-daring corsetted look to Fennell’s sleek ensemble, this red carpet was full of daring ensembles that differed greatly from the look before.

Check out which stars arrived at the Los Angeles premiere of Saltburn below:

  • Margot Robbie

    Margot Robbie turned heads in this corsetted, velvet gown from Schiaparelli.

  • Barry Keoghan

    Barry Keoghan rocked a dring black-and-white Givenchy look for the premiere.

  • Daisy O’Dell

    The m.i.d.s singer Daisy O’Dell looked sensational in this Bronx and Banco sparkling cutout gown.

  • Loey Lane

    YouTuber Loey Lane looked gorgeous in this ensemble consisting of a high-slit, black mesh skirt, and a corsetted top.

  • Sophie Ellis-Bextor

    Singer Sophie Ellis-Bextor looked so stunning in this shimmering, blue Julia Clancey mini-dress.

  • Carey Mulligan

    Carey Mulligan looked so chic in this black Balmain ensemble of a sparkling skirt and an off-the-shoulder suit top.

  • Emerald Fennell

    Emerald Fennell looked oh-so-chic in this caped Valentino gown.

  • Kumail Nanjiani & Emily V. Gordon

    Kumail Nanjiani and Emily V. Gordon looked fantastic together in their dark looks, with Nanjiani in a chocolate brown suit and Gordon rocking a pleated leather Jo Kilda dress.

  • Jacob Elordi

    Jacob Elordi’s caramel, color-shifting suit was one of the favorite looks of the night.
