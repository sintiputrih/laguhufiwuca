Jennifer Chan - InStyle

Publish date: 2024-09-22

Title: E-commerce Writer/Editor

Education: Pepperdine University

Expertise: Fashion, Beauty , Pop Culture

  • Jennifer Chan is an e-commerce writer covering fashion, beauty, and travel shopping content for InStyle, People, and Travel + Leisure.
  • She has covered entertainment events including the Emmys, Oscars, Grammys, Golden Globes, and the SAG Awards.

Experience

As a beauty and fashion editor for over 15 years, Jennifer has covered entertainment events, including the Emmys, Oscars, Grammys, Golden Globes, and the SAG Awards, interviewed A-list celebrities, and planned and created shopping content for multiple publications. She has been published in InStyle, People, Travel + Leisure, and Real Simple among others. She previously worked as the senior editor for Ipsy, where she executed all the copy and brand voice content for emerging beauty and skin care brands.

Jennifer frequently appears on-air as a style and beauty expert for Access Hollywood, Extra, The List, E! News, Sky News, MSNBC, and BET. She has also worked with major retailers like Bloomingdale’s, Macy’s, and Nordstrom as a fashion consultant.

Education

Jennifer received a bachelor’s degree in journalism from Pepperdine University.
