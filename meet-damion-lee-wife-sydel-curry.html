Damion Lee is a professional basketball player for the Phoenix Suns of the National Basketball Association. He transferred to Louisville for his last year of eligibility after three seasons of collegiate basketball at Drexel University. In 2022, Lee and the Golden State Warriors captured the NBA title.

Damion Lee bio

Lee graduated from Calvert Hall College High School in Towson, Maryland, where he was named to the first team of the Baltimore Catholic League and the second team of the Baltimore Sun All-Metro during his senior year.

In the 2010–11 season, Lee played prep basketball for St. Thomas More in Oakdale, Connecticut, where he averaged 17 points, 6 rebounds, and 5 assists per game.

Lee led his team to a 30-7 record and the National Prep School Championship while being named to the First Team All-New England Preparatory School Athletic Council.

Meet Damion Lee wife, Sydel Curry
