Joseph Robert Theismann was born on September 9, 1949, in New Brunswick, New Jersey, USA, to Olga Tobias, of Hungarian ancestry, and Joseph John Theismann, of Austrian descent. He was the son of Olga Tobias and Joseph John Theismann. A former American football player, he has worked as a sports commentator, a corporate speaker, a restaurant, and a part-time actress. He is most well-known for his time as the quarterback of the Washington Redskins of the National Football League, where he played for eight seasons (NFL).
