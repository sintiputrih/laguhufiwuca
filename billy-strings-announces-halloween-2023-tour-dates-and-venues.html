Billy Strings Announces Halloween 2023 Tour Dates And Venues

Publish date: 2024-04-25

Billy Strings, the bluegrass musician, has announced his Halloween 2023 Tour Dates and Venues. The tour will consist of eight shows across three cities in the United States. The venues for most of the shows have not been specified yet, but the cities and dates have been confirmed.

The tour will kick off in Asheville, North Carolina from February 16 to 18, 2024. Billy Strings will then move on to perform in Nashville from February 23 to 25, and end the tour in Atlanta on March 1 and 2. Fans of the musician are eagerly anticipating the announcement of the venues for the shows.

This tour will follow the Van Andel Scramble that will take place in 2023. Billy Strings has been gaining popularity in recent years for his unique blend of traditional bluegrass and modern influences. His upcoming album, ME/AND/DAD, is set to release soon, and the tour dates coincide with the album’s release. Fans can expect a mix of old and new songs, as well as a lively and energetic performance from the musician and his band.

Billy Strings Halloween 2023 Tour Dates

Billy Strings has announced his Halloween 2023 tour dates, and fans are excited to see him perform live once again. The tour will feature a wrestling-themed Halloween show dubbed the ‘Van Andel Scramble’ in Grand Rapids, Michigan, on October 31st. The show is expected to be one of the highlights of the tour, and fans can’t wait to see what Billy Strings has in store for them.

October Dates

The Halloween show in Grand Rapids is just the beginning of the tour. Billy Strings will be performing in several other cities throughout the month of October, including Stateline, Nevada’s Tahoe Event Center on October 8th, West Valley City, Utah’s Maverik Center on October 10th, and more. Fans can check out the full list of October tour dates and venues on JamBase.

November Dates

After a short break, Billy Strings will be back on the road in November, with shows scheduled in several cities throughout the US, UK, and Europe. Some of the highlights of the November tour include shows at London’s O2 Academy Brixton on November 14th and Paris’s L’Olympia on November 23rd. Fans can check out the full list of November tour dates and venues on Billy Strings’ official website.

Overall, Billy Strings’ Halloween 2023 tour promises to be one of the most exciting tours of the year. Fans can expect to hear some of their favorite songs, as well as some new material from Billy Strings’ upcoming album ME/AND/DAD. With so many great shows to choose from, fans are sure to have a great time at any of the tour dates.

Tour Venues

Billy Strings has announced the venues for his Halloween 2023 Tour in the United States. The tour will include shows on both the East and West Coast. Here is a breakdown of the venues by region:

Venues in the East Coast

The East Coast leg of the tour will kick off on October 26th at the Brooklyn Bowl in New York City. The tour will continue with shows at the Fillmore in Philadelphia on October 27th and 28th, and at the 9:30 Club in Washington D.C. on October 29th. The tour will then head south with shows at the Variety Playhouse in Atlanta on November 1st, and at the Ryman Auditorium in Nashville on November 3rd and 4th.

Venues in the West Coast

The West Coast leg of the tour will begin on November 8th at the Crystal Ballroom in Portland, Oregon. The tour will then head to the Showbox SoDo in Seattle on November 9th, and to the Warfield in San Francisco on November 11th and 12th. The tour will then wrap up with shows at the Wiltern in Los Angeles on November 15th and 16th.

Billy Strings is known for his high-energy live performances, and fans can expect an unforgettable experience at each of these venues. Be sure to check the official Billy Strings website for ticketing information and updates on any additional shows that may be added to the tour.
