Zaira Wasim is a remarkable Indian former actress. Zaira Wasim gained international fame with her roles in the films Dangal, Secret Superstar, and  The Sky Is Pink.

Early Life

Zaira Wasim was born on 23 October 2000 and her current age is 21 years old. Zaira hails from Srinagar, Jammu-and-Kashmir, India, and her zodiac sign is Scorpio. She comes from a Muslim family. Moreover, her parents are her father Zahid, and mother Zarqa Wasim.

Zaira’s father is an Executive Manager at J&K Bank, Srinagar, whereas her mother is a teacher.

Education

Talking about her educational journey, Zaira completed her 10th grade at St. Paul’s International Academy in Sonwar, Srinagar.

Career and Professional Life

Caption: Zaira appearing on the poster of the film ‘Dangal’. Source: Feminism in India

Professionally, Zaira Wasim was a popular actress in the Bollywood film industry. Zaira has bagged numerous accolades. The former actress’s awards are a Filmfare Award and a National Film Award.

Likewise, the former actress was honored with the National Child Award for Exceptional Achievement. Ram Nath Kovind presented Zaira with this award at a ceremony in New Delhi in the year 2017. Ram Nath is the President of India.

Zaira made her acting debut with Dangal in the year 2016. In this film, Zaira portrayed the young character, Geeta Phogat. Moreover, Dangal is a biographical sports film that emerged as the highest-grossing Indian film.

This popular film also stars other well-known actors like Aamir Khan, Sakshi Tanwar, Fatima Sana Shaikh, Sanya Malhotra, Suhani Bhatnagar, Aparshakti Khurana, and Girish Kulkarni in the lead roles.

More Details

Caption: Zaira posing for a photo with the lead actors of ‘The Sky Is Pink’. Source: DNA India

Additionally, the young former actress appeared in the role of an aspiring singer in Secret Superstar. This film was released in 2017. Moreover, Secret Superstar became the highest-grossing Hindi film with a female protagonist.

Both Secret Superstar and Dangal were backed by Aamir Khan Productions. For her role in Dangal, Zaira earned the National Film Award for “Best Supporting Actress”. Likewise, for Secret Superstar, Zaira won the Filmfare Critics Award for “Best Actress”.

Likewise, Zaira’s last film appearance was in The Sky Is Pink. This film was released on 11 October 2019. Further, this film is the biopic of Aisha Chaudhary.

Aisha was an Indian motivational speaker, who was a 19-year-old girl when she passed away from pulmonary fibrosis. This film co-stars Priyanka Chopra and Farhan Akhtar. Moreover, for her role as Aisha Chaudhary in this film, Zaira received a nomination for the Filmfare Award for “Best Supporting Actress”.

Then on 30 June 2019, Zaira announced that she would leave her acting career. She did so due to the profession’s conflict with her religious beliefs. Then in November 2020, the young former actress requested fans to take down her pictures from social media. It was because the former actress was trying to start a new chapter in her life.
