Who is Ann Cusack?

Ann Cusack is an American actress.

People know Ann Cusack for her roles in television series including ‘One Tree Hill’, ‘Grey’s Anatomy’, ‘Ghost Whisperer’, ‘Boston Legal’, ‘Criminal Minds’, and ‘Better Call Saul’ among others.

Ann Cusack: Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity, Education

Cusack was born in Brooklyn, New York City, New York on May 22, 1961.

She was born to parents Ann Paula “Nancy” and Dick Cusack.

Her father was an actor, producer, and writer while her mother is a former mathematics teacher and political activist.

Throughout her childhood years, Ann grew up in Evanston, Illinois.

Additionally, she has two sisters: Joan, Susie, and two brothers: John, and Bill.

Cusack became interested in the world of acting during her childhood years.

She is of American nationality. Furthermore, she belongs to the Irish ethnic background.

Talking about her education, Cusack attended the Piven Theatre Workshop.

In addition, she was also enrolled at the Berklee College of Music in Boston, Massachusetts.

Ann Cusack: Career, Salary, Net Worth

Ann Cusack initially starred in her first film role in 1992’s ‘A League of Their Own.’ Additionally, in the same year, she had roles in ‘Overexposed’ and ‘The Jackie Thomas Show.’

In 1993, she appeared as Katherine in the TV series ‘Love & War.’

In the same year, she also appeared in ‘Malice’ and ‘Victim of Love: The Shannon Mohr Story.’

Since then, she has appeared in numerous other movies and television series. All in all, she has over 80 credits as an actress.

Some other movies and television series that Cusack has appeared in are ‘Castle Rock’, ‘Better Call Saul’, ‘Heart, Baby’, ‘And Mr. Mercedes’, ‘Tycoon’, ‘Sully’, ‘Castle’, ‘Backstrom’, ‘Fargo’, ‘Nightcrawler’, ‘Masters of Sex’, ‘Hart of Dixie’, ‘Body of Proof’, ‘Private Practice’, ‘The Genesis Files’, ‘Criminal Minds’, ‘Love at First Hiccup’, ‘The Unit’, ‘Ace Ventura: Pet Detective Jr.’, ‘Little Britain USA’, ‘The Neighbor’, ‘Brothers & Sisters’, ‘Cake: A Wedding Story’, ‘Close to Home’, ‘The Sensation of Sight’, ‘Fatal Contact: Bird Flu in America’, ‘Ghost Whisperer’, ‘The Brotherhood of Poland, New Hampshire’, ‘Star Trek: Enterprise’, ‘America’s Sweethearts’, and ‘What About Joan’ among others.

She won the Commitment to Chicago Award (Chicago Film Critics Association Awards) in 2000.

Cusack has not revealed her current salary. However, she has an estimated net worth of around $20 million as of 2023.
