Professional life

Career-wise Felipe Caicedo is a popular football player in international and club football. He has made a name from his amazing gameplay and performance today. He used to play football from very early years in his country and wanted to have a great career.

After having some experience in high school, and college, he got a chance to play professional football with the team, FC Basel, during the Swiss 2005-2006 season, and he was seventeen years old only at the time.

After playing some time with the Swiss team, he joined Manchester City for four and a half year deal through a $5.2 million transfer fee.

This transaction was recorded as one of the highest transfers in the history of the Swiss Super League. He made his debut with Manchester City against Manchester United on 10 February 2008.

He was later loaned to join Malaga CF rejecting Hall City. Then he later completed a move to freshly promoted side Levante UD for the 2010-11 season. Again after playing briefly for Levante, he joined Lokomotiv Moscow on 25 July 2011 and made his debut against the Volga.
