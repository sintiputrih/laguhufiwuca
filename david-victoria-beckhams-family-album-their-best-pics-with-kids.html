David, Victoria Beckhams Family Album: Their Best Pics With Kids

Publish date: 2024-03-03

David Beckham and Victoria Beckham tied the knot in 1999, started growing their family that same year and have been documenting their parenting journey ever since.

After their eldest son Brooklyn’s birth, the couple went on to welcome sons Romeo and Cruz and daughter Harper. They are both “really hands-on” when it comes to parenting, Victoria told Harper’s Bazaar in January 2020. “

“Yes, I’m up early working out, but one of us will do the school run and we always try to be home by dinnertime,” she said. “We’re very strict about that when we’re in London. At 6 p.m. we are all together eating dinner [and] talking about our days. We communicate a lot.”
