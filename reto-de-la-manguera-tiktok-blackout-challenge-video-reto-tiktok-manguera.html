Kim Kardashian and longtime friend La La Anthony don bathrobes as they lip sync in fun TikTok video

Publish date: 2024-04-28

Kim Kardashian and her friend La La Anthony lip synced over a video call to the tune of Ice Spice’s hit single In Ha Mood in a TikTok video on Friday.

In the clip, the 42-year-old reality TV star and her longtime pal, 40, donned bathrobes and sunglasses while rocking out to the rapper’s song.

The SKIMS founder – who recently joined James Corden in a Carpool Karaoke segment – showed La La on FaceTime as the duo lip synced in alternating verses.

Later that evening, the media personality’s eldest daughter North, 9, upstaged Kim by posted a video with Ice Spice, 23, herself.

To the tune of the Bronx native and PinkPantheress’ song Boy’s a liar Pt. 2, North and two of her friends lip synced and danced with the hitmaker.

Kim sported a beige, plush robe in her clip, but her hair and makeup was still in full glam.

The socialite slicked back her raven black hair into a sleek bun and rocked a muted pink monochromatic makeup look.

Her actress pal wore a white, fleece robe and had her deep red tresses in a straight hairstyle.

Like Kim, she did a subtle smokey eye makeup look that she later hid behind a large pair of shades.

The Keeping Up With The Kardashians star appeared to be sitting in a dark room decorated with various bears.

In the video, Kim lip synced along to the lyrics reading: ‘She a baddie, she know she a ten.’

Then Kim turned to show La La singing: ‘She a baddie with her baddie friend.’

Hours later, North showed up her mother by making a TikTok clip with Ice Spice – the artist behind both songs.

North and her two gal pals wore graphic white T-shirts in the video.

Meanwhile the rapper donned a hot pink, figure-hugging tracksuit, a plush pink purse, and matching sneakers.

The clip showed the girls in various settings as they sang along to the bubblegum-pop hit.

The video started off with North’s friends lounging on the daybed while Ice Spice sits behind them, scrolling on her phone and sing: ‘But I don’t sleep enough without you.’

Later, North joins in as they pose in front of an open fridge and lip sync: ‘And I can’t eat enough without you.’

The entire group then gathers together in front of the camera to sing and dance to the chorus together.

The video was simply captioned with an ice cube and a spicy pepper emoji to represent their special guest. 

North and Kim – who shares a joint TikTok account – often shares fun clips with each other.

Kim shares North as well as her other daughter Chicago, five, and sons Saint, seven, and Psalm, three, with ex-husband Kanye West, 45.
