Publish date: 2024-01-17

The Founder and Leader of Resurrection Power New Generation Church (RPNGC), Rev. Anthony Kwadwo Boakye is confirmed dead.

According to a Graphic online report, the renowned man of God passed away on Tuesday night February 21, 2023, at the Korle-Bu Teaching Hospital. His church has since issued a statement confirming the development.

Rev Boakye suffered from a stroke in November 2021 leaving him bedridden. He subsequently sought extensive medical care at private facilities before he was referred to Korle Bu Teaching Hospital at a point where he finally passed away.
