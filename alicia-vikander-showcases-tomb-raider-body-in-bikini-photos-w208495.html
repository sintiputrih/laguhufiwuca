Alicia Vikander Showcases 'Tomb Raider' Body in Bikini: Photos

Publish date: 2024-10-01

She’s ready to rock Lara Croft’s short shorts! Alicia Vikander showed off her toned Tomb Raider–ready body in a teeny, white bikini while vacationing in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil, on Sunday, May 29.

The newly minted Oscar winner, 27, who has signed on to play Lara Croft in the upcoming Tomb Raider reboot, flaunted her lithe frame in the monotone two-piece featuring a strapless top with woven details and cheeky bikini bottoms. She slicked her brunette tresses up into a ponytail and modeled minimal makeup.

Vikander was in Brazil to attend Louis Vuitton’s Cruise 2017 fashion show, which was hosted at the Niteroi Contemporary Art Museum on Saturday. The brand ambassador for the label enjoyed the presentation from her front-row seat next to legendary French actress Catherine Deneuve (who also poses for LV).
