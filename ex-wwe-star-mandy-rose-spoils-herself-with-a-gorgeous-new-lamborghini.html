Ex-WWE Star Mandy Rose Spoils Herself with a Gorgeous New Lamborghini

Publish date: 2024-03-30

Former WWE Superstar Mandy Rose has been making significant strides in her career and personal life since her departure from the company. After her WWE contract was terminated due to content posted behind premium paywall, Rose has been thriving in her endeavors.

Despite the circumstances surrounding her WWE exit, Mandy Rose has been openly highlighting her successful ventures and showcasing her strong work ethic. One of her recent accomplishments includes purchasing a new 2024 Lamborghini Urus S.

Her fiancé, Tino Sabbattelli, shared video on his Instagram story, revealing the new car and showcasing its interiors. The Lamborghini Urus is luxury SUV with a starting price that ranges from $241,843 to $273,880, depending on trim and options. This purchase reflects Mandy Rose’s financial success and ability to make such an extravagant acquisition.

Mandy Rose’s life has taken a positive turn since her WWE departure, and she has expressed conflicting feelings about potential return to WWE in the future. Regardless of her wrestling career, she has many exciting opportunities to look forward to in her life.
