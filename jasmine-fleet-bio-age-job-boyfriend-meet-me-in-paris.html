Jasmine Fleet On Meet Me In Paris

Meet Me In Paris is Roku Channel’s unscripted feature-length romantic comedy. The show has been dubbed “The Hills meets Emily in Paris”. One of the cast members in the show was Jasmine Fleet who along with Mimi Shou, and Danielle Mateo flew to Paris for a very unconventional shot at love.

The three women had a 10-day French adventure. All three girls spoke of their current standing with their partner to EOnline. Mimi A New York-based jewelry designer sparked a romance with John, a pilot based in Utah. “We do care about each other,” Mimi said about where things stand. “He’s still in Utah, so the long-distance thing is an issue.”

Danielle, another cast member, sparked a romance with basketball player Steve. They ended things with a kiss on the court in the shadow of the Eiffel Tower, things haven’t been easy for L.A.-based Danielle and New York-based Steve. “For Steve and I going into this, those were conversations that we had because we were in two very different locations,” Danielle said. “We’re on opposite ends of the country. That’s not to say New York is very far of a flight.”

As for Jasmine, she left France without a prospective partner. She told E News!, “That experience was at such a critical time in my journey of being newly single and figuring out who I am as a person. Having the support of the ladies? It made a difference. I couldn’t have done it without them.”

Jasmine added, “Building that friendship with these two ladies, that was the prize for me.” The Roku channel romance launched in February. The show’s trailer, which reveals what to expect from the film, has also been revealed.

In fact, all three cast members appeared on Miss Universe 2023 (71st Miss Universe New Orleans) where they promoted the show together.

Reese Witherspoon’s Hello Sunshine and Zoe Saldana’s Cinestar are the producers of Meet Me in Paris, a unique approach to an unscripted rom-com film. The unscripted movie, which lasts roughly 90 minutes, tells the tale of three single friends who travel to Paris on the trip of a lifetime. They are looking for true love for two weeks.

The Roku Channel and Jasmine jointly announced, “J’adore! Join Jasmine on her journey for love 🇫🇷 #MeetMeInParis premieres on February 10!” She previously announced, “In Paris, anything could happen. Stream #MeetMeInParis free on @therokuchannel on February 10! ✨ THE COUNTDOWN BEGINS!!!!”
