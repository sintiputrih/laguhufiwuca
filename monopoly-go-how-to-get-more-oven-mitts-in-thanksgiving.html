Monopoly Go How to Get More Oven Mitts in Thanksgiving Partners

Publish date: 2024-01-20

Oven Mitts are essential to completing Thanksgiving Partners, the latest partner event that is running on Monopoly Go from the 21st-26th November. During that time you need to collect these gloves to get points for the partner event. There are a number of ways to get more mitts, here's everything that we know.

Monopoly Go How to Get More Oven Mitts in Thanksgiving Partners

Remember to check out our complete Thanksgiving Partners guide too for a complete list of rewards for the event and our top tips to choosing partners.

How to Get More Oven Mitts in Thanksgiving Partners

Free Mitt Links

End of Event Update: In the end there were no free mitts links given. And we are as disappointed as a lot of you at that because we didn't get to complete the event. However that's more o do with a normally reliable partner not really contributing much this time around.

On our Free Mitts Links page you can find out why we think there are no mitts links yet and when we think we'll get them.

Quick Wins

Quik Wins refresh daily, so throughout the duration of Thanksgiving Partners there will be opportunities to get more oven mitts by completing Quick Wins. Quick Wins are great at the moment, there is also a five-star purple sticker pack to be won and the big reward of a 5-star golden purple sticker pack for completing a week's worth of Quick Wins.

Board Collection

There are loads of oven mitts scattered around the board, you can't miss them - they are massive, land on any of these and you'll get the number of mitts which is the size of your multiplier. So it's not the best way to target mitts, but it's a decent consolation prize if you just missed an event or tournament tile you may have been aiming for.

Find some Oven Mitts on the Board

Main Events

Main events are probably the biggest and best source of oven mitts, they are easy enough to get as you work your way through the initial milestones, but getting through the later milestones is always tough, so those mitts may be a step too far for a lot of players. Take a look at the current main event's rewards list to see what's available and where - Epic Myths Rewards and Milestones. In total there are thousands available to be won in this main event alone.

Tournaments

Tournaments will also give out oven mitts. The current tournament is Olympian Odyssey on that page you can see the milestone list which shows you all of the mitts that you can get in this tournament and also the leaderboard rewards which give you free mitts and where you need to place at the end of the tournament to get some.

Look out for another tournament starting later today when the current one finishes.

Shop Rewards

Every eight hours check the shop rewards by going to the three lines at the top of the main game screen, selecting shop, then scrolling the top panel to the right until you get to the end. Sometimes as part of the reward package there are oven mitts.

Partner Wheel Spin

One final way to get oven mitts is for a partner to land on the partner part of the wheel at that point you'll be bale to collect some mitts for their efforts. The number of mitts equates to the number of mitts they used on that spin.

With 320,000 points to complete all four pies, you'll need a ton of mitts if you want to complete Thanksgiving Partners.
